A Learning Platform For All

We are a passionate team of educators, technologists, designers, and advocates united by a single goal: to break down the barriers that prevent millions from accessing quality education. Whether it's a student with a visual impairment, a child in a remote village, or a non-native speaker navigating a new language.

  • Non-native language learners
  • Users with hearing or visual impairments
  • Educators pursuing inclusive teaching tool
